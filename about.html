Who We Are

Balaji Technical Designing and Consultancy (BTDC), established in 2079 B.S., is a trusted engineering and consultancy firm providing expert property valuations, technical audits, and infrastructure solutions. Led by qualified and experienced professionals, BTDC specializes in residential, commercial, and land valuations for banks, financial institutions, and private clients.

Our services include highway engineering, irrigation systems, water supply and sanitation projects, urban planning, insurance surveyor works, and technical audits. BTDC is committed to accuracy, reliability, and timely delivery, following industry best practices while promoting sustainable and innovative solutions.

With a client-centric approach, skilled team, and robust quality assurance, BTDC ensures precise, comprehensive, and actionable reports that empower informed decision-making and support successful project outcomes.
