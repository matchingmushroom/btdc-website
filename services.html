Property Valuation Services

BTDC offers a wide range of valuation services designed to meet the specific needs of financial institutions. Our team brings deep expertise in assessing residential and commercial properties, as well as land parcels, delivering accurate and reliable valuations.

  • Residential Property Valuations: Accurate market value assessments for houses, apartments, and condominiums, considering location, size, condition, and comparable sales.
  • Commercial Property Valuations: Comprehensive analysis of office buildings, retail spaces, and industrial complexes, factoring in market trends, income potential, and lease agreements.
  • Land Parcel Valuations: In-depth evaluation of land for development, investment, or financing, considering zoning, location, and market conditions.
  • Financing Valuations: Essential property assessments for banks to determine collateral value for loans.
  • Insurance Valuations: Detailed reports for replacement and insurable values to ensure adequate coverage and risk management.

Insurance Surveyor & Technical Audit Services

BTDC offers professional insurance surveyor and technical audit services, ensuring precise evaluation of properties, assets, and construction projects. Our experts conduct risk assessments, replacement value calculations, and compliance audits, providing reliable reports for insurance coverage, claims, and project verification.

  • Risk Assessments: Comprehensive evaluation of potential risks associated with properties and assets.
  • Replacement Value Calculations: Accurate estimation of replacement costs for insurance purposes.
  • Compliance Audits: Verification of adherence to regulatory requirements and industry standards.
  • Construction Quality Audits: Review of construction quality, design integrity, and contractual obligations.
  • Claims Verification: Independent assessment of insurance claims for accuracy and fairness.

Technical Consultancy & Engineering Services

BTDC provides expert technical consultancy across multiple engineering domains, including highway engineering, irrigation systems, water supply, sanitation, and urban planning. Our experienced engineers design, plan, and supervise infrastructure projects, ensuring efficiency, sustainability, and compliance with industry standards.

  • Highway Engineering: Design, planning, and supervision of road and highway infrastructure projects.
  • Irrigation Systems: Development of efficient irrigation solutions for agricultural and commercial use.
  • Water Supply & Sanitation: Planning and implementation of clean water and sanitation projects.
  • Urban Planning: Comprehensive urban development and land-use planning services.
  • Construction Supervision: On-site supervision to ensure quality, timelines, and budget adherence.
  • Project Feasibility Studies: Detailed analysis of project viability, costs, and potential risks.
